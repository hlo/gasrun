GASRUN

Mobile Gas Trip Calculator

Smartphone friendly trip calculator estimates how much you will spend on gas for your next trip to that restaurant on the outskirts of town you and your significant other love, that road trip with your friends every summer, that weekday grocery run, or (just for kicks) that daily commute to work

By providing tomorrow's gas forecast today, we want to help you make informed decisions that will save you money.

Features

  • today's average gas price in your city
  • forecast tomorrow's gas trend
  • editable gas price
  • proximity based destination auto-complete powered by Google
  • expansive list of vehicle make, model and year
  • remembers your vehicle
  • realistic customer provided car mpg (none of those flawed EPA ratings from maker)
  • keep a record of your trips and accumulative gas cost for budgeting!
Helping save money, one run at a time!

Feature suggestions and feedback are always welcome, send us an email :)
